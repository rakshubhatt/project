Our Facilities

At Patanjali School, we provide an array of facilities to ensure our students have access to the best resources for their academic and personal growth.

Library

Our state-of-the-art library is stocked with a wide range of books, journals, and digital resources to support students in their academic pursuits.

Science and Computer Labs

We have modern science and computer labs equipped with the latest technology to provide hands-on learning experiences.

Sports Grounds

Our well-maintained sports grounds cater to various sports including football, cricket, basketball, and more, promoting physical fitness and teamwork.

Art and Music Rooms

Our dedicated art and music rooms provide a creative space for students to explore and develop their artistic talents.

Health and Wellness Center

Our health and wellness center is staffed with qualified professionals to support the physical and mental well-being of our students.

Other Facilities

We also offer a cafeteria with nutritious meals, transportation services, and a secure environment with CCTV surveillance and security personnel.
